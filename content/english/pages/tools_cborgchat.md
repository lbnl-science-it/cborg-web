#### CBorg Chat

CBorg Chat is an online chat portal that supports live chat-based interaction with all of our models.

Access CBorg Chat at [https://chat.cborg.lbl.gov](https://chat.cborg.lbl.gov).

**Note:** CBorg Chat can also be installed on your phone, enabling you to use vision models with the phone's camera.

**Android App Install**: Navigate to CBorg Chat in Chrome. Click the '...' menu in the top right corner then select 'Add to Home Screen'

**IPhone App Install**: Navigate to CBorg Chat in Safari. Tap 'Share' then 'Add to Home Screen'.

CBorg Chat is based on [Librechat](https://www.librechat.ai/).
